About SF Admin Tools

Built by Salesforce admins, for Salesforce admins

Our Mission

At SF Admin Tools, we understand the daily challenges Salesforce administrators face. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to creating powerful Chrome extensions that streamline workflows, save time, and reduce the complexity of managing Salesforce orgs.

Our team consists of experienced Salesforce professionals who have walked in your shoes. We've felt the frustration of clicking through multiple setup pages, the tedium of repetitive tasks, and the pressure of managing complex security models. These experiences drive us to build better tools.

Our Core Values

User-Centric Design

Time is Money

Our tools are designed to save you hours every week, letting you focus on strategic initiatives.
